📋 How to Play

  • Come-out roll: First roll of a new round. 7 or 11 wins Pass Line. 2, 3, or 12 loses Pass Line ("craps").
  • Point: If come-out roll is 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, or 10, that number becomes the point.
  • Point phase: Keep rolling until you hit the point (win Pass Line) or roll 7 (lose Pass Line, "seven out").
  • Odds Bets: After point is established, add odds to Pass/Don't Pass/Come/Don't Come. These pay true odds with no house edge!
  • Max Odds: 5x on 6/8, 4x on 5/9, 3x on 4/10
  • True Odds: 6/8 pay 6:5, 5/9 pay 3:2, 4/10 pay 2:1
