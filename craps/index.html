Pass Line

Bet on the shooter to win. Wins on 7 or 11 on come-out roll. Loses on 2, 3, or 12.

Any Craps

One-roll bet that wins on 2, 3, or 12. Pays 7:1.

📋 How to Play

  • Come-out roll: First roll of a new round. 7 or 11 wins Pass Line. 2, 3, or 12 loses Pass Line ("craps").
  • Point: If come-out roll is 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, or 10, that number becomes the point.
  • Point phase: Keep rolling until you hit the point (win Pass Line) or roll 7 (lose Pass Line, "seven out").
  • Come/Don't Come: Can only be placed after a point is established. Work like Pass/Don't Pass for subsequent rolls.
  • One-roll bets: Any 7 and Any Craps are resolved on the next roll only.
